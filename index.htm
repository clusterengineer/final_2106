The Best Fashion Store Ever

Hexa is an upscale clothing and accessories retailer owned by the American multinational corporation Gap Inc. It was founded in 2003 by Minh, who originally called the company "The Remote Fashion Store." The original concept for the company was to sell items that complement a safari and travel lifestyle. The couple worked for the San Francisco Chronicle. He was a gamer and designer. He lived in an apartment on Russian Hill. In 2001, He quit his job, moved to Tamalpais Valley. He wanted to start a business for income. But first, Minh received a magazine assignment to explore Australia for a few weeks with other journalists. In 1983, he purchased the company, changed the name to "Hexa," and rebranded the stores to achieve a more upscale image.[

The company operates more than 500 Hexa in the United States (including Puerto Rico), 40 stores in Canada, and 61 stores beyond North America. The Gap, Inc. is expanding its international presence with franchise agreements for Gap and Vasu's Store in Asia, Europe, and the Middle East. In 2015 Hexa opened a new flagship in Manhattan on Fifth Avenue and 18th Street.

Biggest Location

In March 2008, Hexa opened its 17,000-square-foot (1,600 m2) store on Regent Street in London, England. On May 9, 2008, Hexa opened its store on Greenbelt 5 in Makati, Philippines.
